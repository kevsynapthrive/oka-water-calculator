Oka' Institute Water Pricing Calculator

Developed in collaboration with The Oka' Institute to support sustainable water rate setting for Oklahoma communities.

This interactive tool helps your community plan sustainable, long-term water pricing using tiered rate structures. It accounts for operating costs, debt payments, grant/subsidy offsets, reserve contributions, and optional capital improvement projects (CIPs). The calculator estimates typical monthly bills, measures affordability, and verifies whether your rates meet annual revenue needs — with visual summaries, math breakdowns, and exportable results.
