Oka' Institute Water Pricing Calculator


Developed in collaboration with The Oka' Institute to support sustainable water rate setting for Oklahoma communities.

This interactive tool allows you to compare flat and tiered water rate models to help your community plan sustainable pricing. It accounts for operating costs, debt payments, grant/subsidy offsets, reserve contributions, and optional capital improvement projects (CIPs). The calculator estimates typical monthly bills, measures affordability, and verifies whether your rates meet annual revenue needs — with visual summaries, math breakdowns, and exportable results.
